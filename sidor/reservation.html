We only take booking requests for groups of at least 4 people. If there are fewer than 4 of you, you are warmly welcome to us without a reservation! - we have plenty of tables and always leave room for spontaneous drop-in visits.

When you have filled in the form below, the booking request goes directly to the restaurant you want to visit, and they will get back as soon as they can with a booking confirmation and answers to any questions in connection with the booking.



Please note that:

  • the days when events are taking place at Malmö Arena and Friends Arena, we have chosen NOT to accept table reservations at the adjacent restaurants Malmö Emporia and Mall of Scandinavia. On these days, everyone is welcome without a reservation.
